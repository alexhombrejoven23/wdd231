📌 01   Site Name

Bolivia Startup Directory

The name was chosen because it directly communicates the site's purpose: a structured, searchable directory focused entirely on Bolivia's startup ecosystem. The word "Startup" is internationally recognized and appeals to both local entrepreneurs and foreign investors exploring emerging Latin American markets.

Suggested domain: boliviastartups.bo  ·  Alternative: boliviastartupdirectory.com

🎯 02   Site Purpose

What this site is for

Bolivia Startup Directory is a web platform that centralizes and organizes information about Bolivian startups across five key sectors: Technology, Fintech, Agriculture, Health, and Education.

  • Offer a browsable, filterable directory of national startups with relevant company data.
  • Give visibility to Bolivia's entrepreneurial ecosystem for investors, partners, and media.
  • Connect entrepreneurs with resources, events, and opportunities in Bolivia's tech scene.
  • Help students and professionals discover job and internship opportunities at startups.

Primary audience: Bolivian entrepreneurs, tech professionals, Latin America-focused investors, and university students looking for startup careers.

💬 03   Visitor Scenarios

Who visits — and what they ask

These questions represent what a typical visitor might be searching for:

Which active Fintech startups are based in Bolivia, and how can I get in touch with them?
I'm a foreign investor — which Bolivian agro startups have recently received funding?
I'm a university student in La Paz — which education or tech startups are hiring interns?
How do I submit my Bolivian startup to the directory to get more visibility?
🎨 04   Color Scheme

The palette

Colors were selected to feel modern and energetic while keeping Bolivia's green identity at the core. Each color has a specific role to ensure visual clarity and accessibility.

Primary Green #1a7a3c — Header, nav, primary buttons, key accents
Light Green #2ea856 — Hover states, active borders, success badges
Dark Green #0f4f26 — Footer, secondary nav, dark text on light
White #ffffff — Cards, content areas, text on dark bg
Off-White #f5faf7 — Page background, alternating sections
Ink #1e2d24 — Body text, headings, startup data labels
✏️ 05   Typography

Fonts & how they're used

Two Google Fonts chosen for contrast of personality — one with flair, one for clarity.

Montserrat — headings & navigation

h1–h3 · nav links · buttons · labels

Bolivia Startup Directory

Innovative Companies Across Five Sectors

Montserrat was chosen for its geometric precision and modern personality — it reads as "tech-forward" and pairs naturally with startup branding. Weight 700 works well as a header over the primary green.

Open Sans — body text

paragraphs · descriptions · startup data · forms

We are a platform that connects Bolivia's entrepreneurial ecosystem with growth opportunities, funding, and national and international visibility.

Open Sans delivers excellent readability at all screen sizes. Its open letterforms make it ideal for long descriptions and data-dense startup cards.
